Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-23

This Privacy Policy describes how "TOEIC Learning" (the "Service" or "App") collects, uses, and protects your data when you use the Service.

1. Data We Collect

When you sign in and authorize via Google, the Service may collect the following data:

  • Basic Google account information: such as your email address and public profile data (e.g. display name and profile picture).
  • Google Drive files authorized to the Service: limited to files created by the Service, or files you explicitly select or grant access to.
  • Learning data you enter in the Service: such as learning progress, settings, and notes (content depends on the Service's features).

2. Google Drive Permission Scope (Important)

The Drive permission used by this Service is https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.appdata, along with basic Google account scopes (userinfo.profile, userinfo.email) to display your signed-in account information. This means:

  • The Service can only access the hidden App Data folder in Google Drive (used for backup and restore within this App).
  • The Service cannot read or list any files in the regular Google Drive file area.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide sign-in and identity verification (e.g. displaying which account is currently signed in).
  • To provide data storage and sync features (e.g. saving your learning progress to your authorized Google Drive files).
  • To maintain and improve the Service (e.g. debugging and enhancing stability).

4. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit request or consent.
  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or governmental authority requests.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and property of the Service, or to prevent unlawful activity.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • If you store data on Google Drive, it is retained within your Google account. You may delete files created or used by the Service at any time directly in Google Drive.
  • If the Service stores data locally in your browser (e.g. localStorage), you may delete it by clearing your browser data.

6. Data Security

We employ reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee that your data will be completely free from unauthorized access, disclosure, or other risks.

7. Children's Privacy

This Service is not directed at children. If you believe a child has provided personal data without parental or guardian consent, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to address it.

8. Third-Party Services

This Service may use Google services (e.g. Google OAuth, Google Drive API). Your use is also governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details.
